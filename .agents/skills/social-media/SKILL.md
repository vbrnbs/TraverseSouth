# Traverse South Social Media & Content Specialist

This skill enforces the social media execution protocols for **Traverse South**, bridging high-end expedition branding with low-overhead execution for a busy founder working a full-time job.

---

## 🎯 1. Brand Core & Copywriting Tone
*   **The Subtitle:** *Creating experiences for adventure lovers.*
*   **The Core Philosophy:** *The adventure is theirs; the execution is ours.* We sell access to true wilderness and multi-day expeditions. Clients get the world's best terrain combined with a zero-admin protocol so they just show up and everything works.
*   **The Tone:** Authoritative, uncompromising, and deeply experiential. Zero fluff, zero generic tourism tropes, zero "resort lobby" language.
*   **Aesthetic Discipline:** **Quiet Luxury / Editorial**. Dark-first, moody, desaturated, high contrast. Deep forest greens, granite grays, glacial blues, and natural textures.

## 🛠️ 2. The "Lean Founder" Workflow (Crawl-Walk-Run)
The founder operates on a strict **52-hour work week** building the business alongside a full-time job. Social media must never cause operational drag.
*   **Frequency:** Start with **1 post per week** per platform. Scale to **2 posts per week** only once a routine is firmly established.
*   **Batching (Sundays - 1 Hour):** Collect raw footage, AI-generate missing B-roll, and write all captions in one session.
*   **Engagement (15 mins/day):** Reply to comments, engage with 5 target operator accounts, and connect with 5 target B2B executive prospects.

## 🧠 3. Platform Intelligence (Social Media Strategist Knowledge)
> For the full deep-dive playbook (algorithm mechanics, ranking signal tables, growth levers, anti-patterns), read [Platform Intelligence Playbook](file:///.agents/skills/social-media/references/platform_intelligence.md).

### Instagram Algorithm — What You Must Know
*   **Separate ranking engines** for Reels, Feed, Stories, and Explore. Reels = discovery (non-followers); Feed = relationship (followers); Stories = retention (inner circle).
*   **Top 3 Signals (confirmed by Adam Mosseri):**
    1.  **Watch Time** — Retention in first 3 seconds is critical. Completion rate and rewatches multiply reach.
    2.  **DM Shares ("Sends Per Reach")** — The single most powerful engagement signal. Design content that makes people send it to a specific friend.
    3.  **Likes Per Reach** — Engagement *efficiency* matters more than total likes.
*   **The Audition System:** Public Reels are shown to a small test group of non-followers first. High performance → wider distribution → potential viral reach.
*   **Strengths:** Visual impact, high per-follower engagement, strong discovery engine via Reels/Explore.
*   **Weaknesses:** Production-intensive, weak for direct B2B conversion, no clickable links in captions, algorithm volatility.
*   **Key Tactics:** Hook in first 2-3 seconds (dramatic visual, not slow fade). Keyword-rich captions > hashtags for discoverability. Geo-tag every post. Pin top 3 posts to grid.

### LinkedIn Algorithm — What You Must Know
*   Shifted from **Social Graph** (who you know) to **Interest Graph** (what you care about). AI system "360Brew" prioritizes expertise and depth.
*   **Distribution:** Post shown to 2-5% of network → if strong engagement in first 60-90 mins → extended to wider interest-matched audience.
*   **Top Signals:**
    1.  **Dwell Time** (Primary) — >15 seconds triggers reach multiplier. <3 seconds causes de-ranking. Long-form text and PDF carousels maximize this.
    2.  **Comment Quality** — One thoughtful comment ≈ 15x the value of one like. LinkedIn NLP evaluates depth; "Great post!" has near-zero impact.
    3.  **Topic Authority** — Post consistently about 2-3 topics to build an algorithmic "fingerprint" that drives targeted distribution.
*   **Strengths:** Unmatched decision-maker access, high-intent professional audience, organic reach still viable, "Knowledge & Advice" content gets 3-5x more reach.
*   **Weaknesses:** Zero tolerance for fluff/promotion, external links reduce reach by up to 60%, weekend posting is dead, higher ad costs.
*   **Key Tactics:** 210-character hook before "see more" fold. Post from founder's personal profile (5-10x reach vs. company page). Reply to every comment within 60 mins. Use DM funnels ("DM me 'EXPEDITION'") instead of links. Cite research data for authority.

### Content Format Rankings
| Rank | Instagram | LinkedIn |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🥇 | **Reels (7-15 sec)** — Highest discovery | **Long-form text (800-1,200 words)** — Max dwell time |
| 🥈 | **Carousels (4-5 slides)** — High engagement | **PDF Carousels** — Swipe-through = dwell time |
| 🥉 | **Stories** — Low reach, high retention | **Native Video (30-90 sec)** — Humanizes brand |
| ⬜ | Single image posts — Lowest reach | Posts with external links — Actively suppressed |

### Optimal Posting Schedule (NZST)
| Platform | Best Days | Best Times | Current Phase |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram** | Tue, Wed, Thu | 9-11 AM or 7-9 PM | 1x/week → 2x/week |
| **LinkedIn** | Tue, Wed, Thu | 8-11 AM | 1x/week → 2x/week |

### Cardinal Rules (Enforced by This Skill)
1.  **Consistency > Frequency.** One post per week, every week, for 6 months beats sporadic bursts.
2.  **Never post filler.** One breathtaking Reel or deeply insightful LinkedIn post > three mediocre posts.
3.  **Repurpose, don't copy.** Same core idea can be a 7-sec Reel AND a 1,000-word LinkedIn post — but adapt format, tone, and hook.
4.  **Engage daily (15 mins).** Comment on 5 target accounts. Reply to every comment on your own posts within 1 hour.
5.  **No external links in LinkedIn post body.** Use "Link in first comment" or DM CTAs.
6.  **No stock photos. Ever.** Use real footage, operator-sourced content, or AI-generated B-roll (with un-AI-ing protocol).
7.  **No weekend posts on LinkedIn.** Engagement drops 70%+.
8.  **No engagement bait.** "Like if you agree!" is penalized on both platforms.

1.  **Monthly Matrix:** Ask *"Build me a 4-week content matrix for [month]."* → Get a balanced plan cycling all 5 pillars with platform routing, hooks, and Reel prompts.
2.  **Raw Dump Converter:** Drop any messy thought or experience mid-week → Get a LinkedIn post + Instagram concept + caption + Reel prompt instantly.
3.  **Science Hook Generator:** Ask *"Give me 3 science-backed LinkedIn hooks"* → Pull from research library (Huberman, Finnish Sauna Study, Creativity in the Wild, etc.).
4.  **Contrarian Take Generator:** Ask *"Give me a contrarian LinkedIn take about [topic]"* → Challenge industry assumptions (e.g., "Spa weekends don't rest executives. Here's what actually works.").
5.  **Seasonal Trigger:** Ask *"What should I post this week given it's [month/season]?"* → Surface content aligned with NZ winter alpinism, Q1 planning season, EOFY, mental health month, etc.

## 📱 5. Platform Execution Protocols

### A. Instagram (The Visual Photo Album & Vibe)
*   **Objective:** Sell the atmosphere, awe, and scale of the Southern Alps. Make viewers feel like they are stepping into the wild.
*   **Content Options (Alternate when posting 1x/week):**
    *   **Option A (Reel - Vibe):** 7-10 second atmospheric clip of NZ wilderness (waterfalls, gorges, helicopters, steam). Sound: Ambient wind, rushing rivers, helicopter blades.
    *   **Option B (Carousel - Experience):** 4-5 slides. Slide 1: Hero shot. Slide 2: Topographic route map. Slides 3-4: Technical gear or sauna close-ups. Slide 5: Minimal CTA.
*   **Stories:** 1-3 times/week. Low-overhead real-time updates, testimonials, and raw behind-the-scenes clips.

### B. LinkedIn (The High-Performance Reset & B2B Offsites)
*   **Objective:** Target high-earning, time-poor executives, founders, and VC/PE partners. Reframe extreme adventure as the ultimate executive clarity tool.
*   **Core Hook:** Discomfort and physical stakes in the wild make you a sharper decision-maker in the boardroom.
*   **Content Pillars:**
    1.  **The "Switch Off" Protocol:** Off-grid expeditions + sauna/ice bath as a hard executive reboot.
    2.  **Wilderness vs. Boardroom:** Parallels between navigating rapids/glaciers and high-stakes business decisions.
    3.  **The Zero-Admin Thesis:** Solving logistical chaos and weather cancellations with our Seamless Pivot.
    4.  **Survival-Grade Team Trust:** True team building where trust is a physical dependency, not a conference room slide.

## 🎬 6. AI Video Generation Rules (The "Un-AI-ing" Protocol)
When generating video prompts for tools like **Google Veo 3.1, Luma Dream Machine, or Kling AI**, you MUST apply these strict prompt engineering rules to eliminate the glossy "AI sheen":
1.  **Kill the Buzzwords:** NEVER use: *photorealistic, hyperrealistic, 8k, masterpiece, cinematic, stunning, beautiful, perfect lighting, CGI*.
2.  **Name Real Hardware:** Always specify exact physical cameras and lenses:
    *   *Shot on ARRI ALEXA Mini with anamorphic lens* (for moody, high-end film feel)
    *   *Shot on DJI Inspire 3 with Zenmuse X9-8K Air* (for aerial drone tracking)
    *   *Shot on 35mm / 16mm Kodak Vision3 film stock* (for raw, grainy analog feel)
    *   *Shot on 50mm f/1.8 prime lens* (for shallow depth of field close-ups)
3.  **Demand Natural Imperfections:** Always explicitly request technical flaws:
    *   *Organic film grain, subtle sensor noise, slight camera breathing, natural handheld micro-movement, soft background focus, diffused natural daylight, overcast flat color profile, muted desaturated earthy tones.*
4.  **Negative Prompts:** When applicable, recommend this negative prompt block:
    *   `CGI, 3D render, Unreal Engine, digital painting, oversaturated, vibrant colors, neon, smooth plastic surfaces, artificial lighting, warping, morphing`
