## <a name="system"></a>3. The Idea Generation System

### Method A — The Monthly Matrix (5 minutes, 4 weeks of ideas)
At the start of each month, say: *"Build me a 4-week content matrix for [month]."*
I will generate a balanced plan cycling through all 5 pillars with platform routing, specific post ideas, and Reel prompts for each week.

### Method B — The Raw Dump Converter
Dump any messy thought, experience, or story mid-week. Examples:
> "Saw a CEO freeze on a boardroom presentation today, made me think about how that never happens after 3 days off-grid."
> "Just read about Huberman's cold protocol."
> "Had a client say the weather pivot was better than the original plan."

I will instantly convert it into:
1. A LinkedIn hook + full post
2. An Instagram concept + caption + Reel prompt

### Method C — The Science Hook Generator
Say: *"Give me 3 science-backed LinkedIn hooks."*
I will draw from the research library in `social_media_strategy.md` and current scientific consensus to generate authoritative, data-driven hooks your target audience cannot scroll past.

### Method D — The Contrarian Take Generator
Say: *"Give me a contrarian LinkedIn take about [topic]."*
The most-shared LinkedIn posts challenge conventional wisdom. Examples for Traverse South:
- "Spa weekends don't rest executives. Here's what actually works."
- "Most corporate team-building is a waste of your budget. Here's the science."
- "The NZ adventure travel industry has a dirty secret: it's built for tourists, not executives."

### Method E — The Seasonal & Event-Triggered Prompt
Say: *"What should I post this week given it's [month/season/event]?"*
I will surface seasonally relevant ideas (e.g., winter alpinism content in June, summer packrafting in December, Davos commentary in January).

### Method F — Competitor & Community Listening
Accounts and hashtags to monitor weekly for inspiration (never copy, only observe patterns):
- **Instagram:** `@newzealand`, `@pure_nz`, `@ems_adventures`, `@wilderness_collective`, `#southernalps`, `#nzadventure`
- **LinkedIn:** Search "executive resilience," "wilderness leadership," "corporate offsites" and observe what gets high comment engagement. Identify gaps you can fill.
