## 🖼️ 2. Carousel Structure (The 5-Slide Story)

When generating Carousel posts (`Option B`), follow this strict 5-slide editorial layout:

*   **Slide 1 (Hero Shot):** A dramatic, high-contrast, desaturated wide photograph of the terrain or activity. *Text overlay: Clean modern font, max 5 words (e.g., "THE DART RIVER EXPEDITION").*
*   **Slide 2 (Topographic Route):** A clean, dark-themed minimalist map or elevation profile of the journey showing key waypoints.
*   **Slide 3 (The Grit/Equipment):** Close-up detail shot of technical alpine gear, packrafts, or helicopter rigging. *Text overlay: Brief technical note on safety & guide lineage.*
*   **Slide 4 (The Reward/Vibe):** A shot of the contrast therapy component (steam rising from a backcountry tub, campsite by firelight, or alpine summit ridge).
*   **Slide 5 (Minimal CTA):** Clean dark canvas (`#0b0b0b`). *Text: "The adventure is yours. The execution is ours. Link in bio to inspect itineraries."*
