# Traverse South: Terms & Conditions and Refund Policy

Welcome to Traverse South. We are an exclusive booking platform designed for adventure lovers. By booking with us, you agree to the following terms, which are designed to protect you, our operators, and the integrity of your expedition.

## 1. Our Role: The Zero-Admin Protocol
Traverse South acts exclusively as an automated booking conduit and logistical management service. We curate, package, and manage the administrative backend of your adventure. 
- **What We Guarantee:** We guarantee a zero-admin experience. We handle the bookings, the scheduling, the payments, and the crisis management if weather disrupts your plans.
- **What We Do Not Do:** We do not operate the helicopters, guide the alpine ascents, or pilot the boats. All physical activities are operated by our vetted, third-party professional partners. 

## 2. Weather Cancellations & The "Seamless Pivot"
Weather is the single biggest variable in New Zealand adventure tourism. When bad weather hits, we do not leave you stranded to figure it out yourself.

- **The Seamless Pivot:** If an operator cancels your activity due to weather or safety concerns, our Ops Team immediately steps in. We will rapidly present you with logistical alternatives (re-scheduling for the next day, swapping to an all-weather activity, etc.) and seamlessly reorganise your itinerary.
- **Weather Refunds:** If a Seamless Pivot is not possible or you choose not to accept the alternative, you will receive a **100% full refund** for the cancelled activity. We process this on your behalf—no chatbots, no holding on the phone.

## 3. Client Cancellations
If *you* need to cancel your booking:
- Because we act as the booking agent for independent operators, **client cancellations are governed strictly by the individual operator’s cancellation policy** (typically 24 to 48 hours prior to departure). 
- If you cancel outside the operator's penalty window, we will process a full refund.
- If you cancel inside the penalty window, the operator's specific cancellation fees will apply. The exact cancellation window for each activity is clearly stated on your booking itinerary.

## 4. Operational Liability & Safety
By booking through Traverse South, you acknowledge and agree that:
- **Operational Liability:** All physical, operational, and safety liability rests entirely and strictly with the third-party operator conducting the activity. 
- **Assumption of Risk:** Adventure tourism inherently involves risk. You agree to follow all safety instructions provided by the operator and sign any required liability waivers presented by them prior to departure.
- Traverse South, its founders, and its agents are not liable for any injury, loss, damage, or delay sustained during the physical execution of the activities.

## 5. Payments & Security
- **Secure Processing:** All transactions are processed via our secure, encrypted headless e-commerce infrastructure. Traverse South does not directly store your credit card or payment information on our servers.
- **Pricing:** All prices are listed in NZD (unless otherwise stated) and include all applicable taxes and booking fees.

## 6. Privacy & Data Handling (NZ Privacy Act & GDPR)
To execute the Zero-Admin Protocol, we must share specific logistical information (e.g., your name, contact details, weight for helicopters, and dietary requirements) with our vetted operators.
- **Strict Necessity:** Your data is used exclusively to curate, book, and safely execute your expedition. We do not sell your data to third-party marketers.
- **Compliance:** We comply strictly with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and align our data handling with global standards, including GDPR for our European clients. 
- **Right to Erasure:** You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold on you or request its deletion after your expedition is completed, provided it does not conflict with our legal or financial record-keeping obligations.

## 7. Real Human Support Guarantee
If something goes wrong on the ground, you are not left to a voicemail or an automated system. You have direct access to our Ops Team, who already have your itinerary context and will intervene immediately to resolve logistical disruptions.
